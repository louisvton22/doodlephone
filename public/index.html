Objective

The goal of the game is get your team's guesser to correctly guess the prompt based on a drawing you and the rest of the drawers on your team will collaborate in making.

  1. When it's your turn, start or add to the drawing on the canvas.
  2. When time's up, the next player on your team will receive your drawing.
  3. Once all your teammates have finished drawing, the drawing will be handed to the guesser.
  4. Whichever guesser guesses more closely to the prompt, that team wins!
